CODE OF HONOR

Respect First.
Every member deserves respect—no ego, no trash talk. Disrespect = warning or exile.
Brotherhood Above All.
No betrayal, no secrets against your brothers or sisters. We stand together.
Loyalty Over Everything.
Once you ride with us, you stay loyal. No switching sides, no double play.
Stay Silent. Stay Solid.
Internal matters stay within the gang. Outsiders don't get our stories.
Act Mature.
Minimum age 18+. Handle yourself with class—no childish drama.

IN-GAME BEHAVIOR

No Toxicity or Harassment.
No racism, sexism, or bullying—ever.
Respect the Tag.
Represent the gang name with pride. Never misuse it.
Active Participation.
Be active in events, missions, and gang activities. Ghosts get replaced.
Obey the Chain of Command.
Follow your leader and co-boss. Every order has a reason.
Protect the Family.
Defend your members in or out of the game. One stands, all stand.

LOYALTY & TRUST

No Betrayal.
Anyone caught leaking into, disrespecting members, or joining rival crews = permanent ban.
Keep Secrets.
What happens in the gang stays in the gang.
Be Reliable.
If you promise, you deliver. Word is bond.
